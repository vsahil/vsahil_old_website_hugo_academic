Reinforcement Learning for Counterfactual Explanations

Sahil Verma, John Dickerson, Keegan Hines
Jun 15, 2021 0 min read Explainability in ML
Illustrative diagram counterfactual explanations. The datapoint labeled X (blue) got classified in the negative class. CF1 (red) and CF2 (green) are two counterfactuals for X, which the model classifies in the positive class. Several counterfactuals can be generated for a datapoint, which differ in closeness to the original datapoint and other desirable properties.
